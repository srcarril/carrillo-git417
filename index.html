About

Members

Amelia Meath &
Nick Sanborn

Electronic pop duo Sylvan Esso has been making music together since 2013 creating hits like “Hey Mami”, “Radio”, and “​Ferris Wheel​”. Sylvan Esso has toured with ODESZA, Flock of Dimes, and Phoebe Bridgers. The duos latest release “No Rules Sandy” was released in August 2022.
